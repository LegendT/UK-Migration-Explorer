## Who

This site is built and maintained by **Anthony George**, trading as Legendary Tone.

It is the companion to the [UK Public Finances Explorer](https://ukpublicfinances.org),
which applies the same method to government debt and borrowing: official statistics,
per-figure provenance, and plain English about what each number does and does not measure.

## Who pays

**Nobody.** This site is unfunded. It is built in spare time and hosted at negligible cost
on static hosting. There is no grant, no advertising, no sponsorship, no commercial
relationship with any organisation working in this field, and no payment from anyone with an
interest in how migration statistics are reported.

If that ever changes, this page changes first, before anything else on the site does.

## What that means for what you read here

Being unfunded is not a claim to neutrality. It removes one category of pressure and leaves
every other one in place: my own assumptions, the sources I happen to know about, and the
questions it does not occur to me to ask.

What is offered instead of a claim to be unbiased is a claim to be **checkable**. Every
figure on this site carries the publication it came from, the period it covers, what it
counts, and when it was last verified. The underlying data files are public, the code that
renders them is public, and the record of every change to a published figure is public. If a
number here is wrong, you should be able to prove it without taking my word for anything.

## What this site is not

It is **not a fact-checking organisation**. It has no editorial board, no second reader, and
no institutional backing. It is one person applying a consistent method, and it should be
read that way.

It is **not a source of original data**. Every figure was published by somebody else, and
where our figure and theirs differ, theirs is right and ours is a bug.

It **takes no position on what immigration policy should be**. That is a question about
values, and this site has no standing to answer it. It takes a firm position on the misuse
of statistics, and applies that in every direction.

## Corrections

If something here is wrong, please say so, and say which figure and what you think it should
be. [Report an error](https://github.com/LegendT/UK-Migration-Explorer/issues/new).

Every change to a published figure is recorded in the
[public changelog](https://github.com/LegendT/UK-Migration-Explorer/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md),
with the reason and the source. See [corrections](/sources-and-method/#corrections) for the
full policy.

## Reuse

The data files and the code are public. Most figures are Crown copyright, published under the
Open Government Licence v3.0.

If you reuse them, keep the source and the period attached. A figure stripped of those is the
raw material for exactly the confusion this site exists to reduce.
